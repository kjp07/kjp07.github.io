My Thoughts On Cheese...

Anyone who knows me, knows I love cheese. Nothing gives me greater satsifaction than dunking a piece of cheddar into
a tomato soup. I've been told many times how odd this is. I say "You don't know what you're missing" and continue dunking.
Grated parmesan on garlic fries gives me joy. Stringy mozarella on pizza makes me smile. You get the idea.

Interesting... Tell me more...

Here are some fun facts about cheese:

  • There are more than 700 cheeses made in the UK.
  • Blue cheese gets it's coloring from an edible mould, such as Penicillium roqueforti and Penicillium glaucum.
  • If you love cheese, you can call yourself a turophile!
  • And if you collect cheese labels, you can also add tyrosemiphile to your title!
