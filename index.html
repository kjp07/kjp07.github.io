The Adventures of Dinky Dino

Chapter 1: First Encounter

I came across Dinky Dino at the bus stop. I had several thoughts when I did.

My immediate thought was that some small child must have dropped her and their parent didn't notice.
Did the child know it had lost their dino friend? Did the dino know it had been abandoned?
My second though was whether I should leave Dinky Dino or take her on an adventure.
Though I suppose work is hardly an advanture, but to a Dinky Dino it could be.
